Walter


Description


Veteran, holy defender, wise, straight, strong, master of our hero. Gave his life to defend all he loves.

Walter was the reference for our hero, he teach him all that he knows. When Walter die, as all the crusaders our hero inherited his weapons and armors to keep going with the holy crusade.


Shape & Silhouette


As we can see, the shape of Walter is between square and round, thanks to his beard this gives us the perception that he is a kind and strong person, symmetrical which helps us to have a perception of trust.

If we look at his posture, we see that he is an upright person and how he carries the sword gives us the feeling that he is carefree, also thanks to the shape of his beard we can see that he is a serious and wise person.


Personality


In order to correctly define the personality of Walter the model of the big five will be used:

  • Openness:

    Walter's character makes him a cautious person, before taking a step he thinks a thousand times to be sure.

  • Conscientuiousness:

    Since Walter is a cautious person, this has a direct impact on his responsibility, making him a very organized person and capable of taking many people under his charge.

  • Extraversion:

    Throughout his life Walter has learned to solve problems by himself and has never had the need to convert them, which is why it makes him a reserved person.

  • Agreeableness:

    Walter has always been a very friendly person, capable of empathizing with all people and thanks to his experience he can give very good advice so that the person can follow their path correctly.

  • Neuroticism:

    Walter is known as a self-confident person, something that infects people around him a lot, feeling very comfortable with him.

As a final conclusion Walter is a cautious, responsible, reserved, friendly and self-confident person. Regarding social needs, if we translate it into a Maslow pyramid, it can be indicated that Walter is self-realized.


Archetype


Following the archetypes of Carl Jung Walter would be the Mentor archetype, because he is the one who has taught the main character everything he knows and is the main teacher of the player.
